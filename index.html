Your Guide to Preparing the Perfect Half-Cooked Poratta

HALF COOKED

KEEP REFRIGERATE: O°C TO 5°C This ensures the parathas stay fresh and safé. Helps prevent spoilage and retains their softness. Always store in the refrigerator to maintain freshness and quality before use.

JUST HEAT

Heat the paratha on a flat pan for about 1 minute on each side until golden brown. 180°C - 200°C (356°F - 392°F) This temperature ensures: Even browning, crispy outer layers, proper heating through the inner folds without burning or drying out.

CLAP IT

Once cooked, place the paratha on a flat surface. Gently press with both hands or clap it to separate and fluff up the layers. This brings out the true softness and layered texture of your poratta.
